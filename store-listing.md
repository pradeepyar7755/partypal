## Full Description (Both Stores)

🎉 Plan the perfect party in minutes — powered by AI!

PartyPal is your all-in-one party planning assistant. Whether you're throwing a birthday bash, wedding celebration, graduation party, or any event, PartyPal's AI creates a complete plan tailored to your vision.

✨ AI-POWERED PLANNING
Tell us your event type, guest count, budget, and theme — and our AI generates a full plan with timeline, checklist, budget breakdown, and vendor recommendations in seconds.

📋 SMART DASHBOARD
• Interactive timeline with milestone tracking
• Auto-generated checklist by category
• Budget breakdown with visual progress bars
• Mood board and theme inspiration

🏪 VENDOR DISCOVERY
• Browse local vendors by category (DJ, catering, photography, decor, and more)
• AI-matched vendor recommendations based on your event
• Save favorites to your shortlist
• Track vendor costs vs. budget in real-time

👥 GUEST MANAGEMENT
• Add guests individually or bulk import
• Track RSVPs (invited, confirmed, declined)
• Send update notifications when plans change
• Share your event with co-planners

🗳️ POLLS & COLLABORATION
• Create polls to let guests vote on themes, dates, or venues
• Share poll links via text or social media
• See results in real-time

💡 WHY PARTYPAL?
• No signup required to explore
• Beautiful, intuitive design
• Works offline after initial load
• Free to use — no hidden fees

Whether it's an intimate dinner party for 10 or a blowout bash for 200, PartyPal makes planning effortless and fun. Start your next event today! 🥳
